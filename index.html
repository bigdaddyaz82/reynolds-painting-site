About Reynolds Painting & Resurfacing LLC

At Reynolds Painting & Resurfacing, we're passionate about delivering high-quality painting and resurfacing services to homes and businesses throughout the Valley. While the company was officially established recently, I've been painting professionally here in the Valley since 2016. As the owner and main painter, I personally take pride in every project, ensuring the best possible service for my clients. I treat every job as if it were my own home—focused on quality, precision, and care.

From residential to commercial properties, no job is too big or too small. My goal is simple: to provide professional, trustworthy service that you can rely on—delivered on time and within budget. We are committed to beating other licensed LLCs on both price and quality.

Reynolds Painting & Resurfacing LLC is locally owned and operated. We offer free estimates and flexible consultation times.

Why Invest in Professional Painting?

  • Boosts Property Value: A fresh coat of paint can significantly increase your home's market value.
  • Enhances Curb Appeal: Make a powerful first impression with a clean, beautifully updated look.
  • Protects Your Surfaces: Quality paint shields against weather, moisture, insects, and daily wear.
  • Improves Indoor Air Quality: Modern low-VOC and zero-VOC paints are healthier for your family.
  • Personalizes Your Space: Reflect your unique style and taste with custom colors and finishes.
  • Cost-Effective Renovation: Painting is one of the most affordable ways to dramatically transform any room or exterior.
