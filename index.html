About Reynolds Painting & Resurfacing LLC

At Reynolds Painting & Resurfacing LLC, our mission is to deliver professional, high-quality painting and resurfacing services that enhance the beauty and value of your property. We are a locally owned and operated business that understands the needs of homeowners and business owners throughout the Valley.

My name is Shaun Reynolds, and I’ve been painting professionally in Arizona since 2016. When I started this company, it was built on one simple principle: I treat every project as if it were my own. That means doing the job right and never cutting corners.

Whether it’s a single room, an entire home, or a commercial property, we bring precision, professionalism, and pride to every job. From detailed interior painting to tough, lasting garage floor epoxy, we have the experience to get it done right the first time.

When you hire Reynolds Painting & Resurfacing, you're not just getting another contractor — you're getting someone who truly cares about the final result. Let’s make your vision a reality.
